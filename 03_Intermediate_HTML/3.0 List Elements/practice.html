Ofrin's Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls


Ingredients


For the Dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the Filling:

  • 2/3 cupdark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions:


  1. Mix the Milk woth the yeast, sugar, eggs
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough
  4. Knead
  5. TRansfer
  6. After
